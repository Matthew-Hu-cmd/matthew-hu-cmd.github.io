This new blog gives the homepage a cleaner structure:

- The homepage stays concise and easy to scan.
- Longer writing now has a dedicated page and permanent links.
- Future posts can be added with plain Markdown in the `_posts/` folder.

For every new article, create a file like `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` under `_posts/` and add front matter such as:

```yaml
---
layout: post
title: "Your post title"
excerpt: "A short summary shown on the blog page."
---
```

That is enough for Jekyll to generate a standalone post page automatically.
